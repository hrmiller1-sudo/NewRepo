Camping in Tennessee

A little bit about camping:

There are several beautiful locations in Tennessee to hike and camp. Around
the Nashville area, one of my favorite spots is Montgomery Bell State Park. The
hike is normally mild to moderate, and one can choose from several options, such as
picnics or backcountry camping.
Another great location in Tennessee is the Virgin Falls. The backcountry camping
here is much longer and more intense compared to Montgomery, but it's worth it
for the views. It's important to remember, however, that you need to be well
prepared for a hike that takes you miles into the woods, hours from major cities.
Be aware of trail condtions and weather during your stay and make sure you
have a means of communication.
